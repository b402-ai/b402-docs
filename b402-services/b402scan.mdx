[b402scan](https://b402scan.ai) is the b402 ecosystem explorer with a unified interface where users can browse buyers (agents) and sellers, make on-chain and off-chain queries, and launch new servers that follow the b402 protocol standards. 

It serves as the gateway to discover and participate in the growing b402 ecosystem. 

The b402scan will be launched soon.
