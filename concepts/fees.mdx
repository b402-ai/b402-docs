The b402 protocol charges a small fee on each transaction, deducted from the payment amount. Buyers never need to pay gas separately — all costs are handled within the transaction.

## Transaction Fees

Fees are tiered by payment volume:

| Tier | Volume | Protocol Fee | Fixed Fee | Total |
|------|--------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Small | Below $1,000 | 0.05% | $0.05 | 0.05% + $0.05 |
| Medium | $1,000 - $10,000 | 0.08% | $0.05 | 0.08% + $0.05 |
| Large | Above $10,000 | 0.10% | $0.05 | 0.10% + $0.05 |

## Examples

| Payment Amount | Protocol Fee | Fixed Fee | Total Fee |
|---------------|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| $100 | $0.05 | $0.05 | $0.10 |
| $500 | $0.25 | $0.05 | $0.30 |
| $1,000 | $0.80 | $0.05 | $0.85 |
| $5,000 | $4.00 | $0.05 | $4.05 |
| $10,000 | $8.00 | $0.05 | $8.05 |
| $25,000 | $25.00 | $0.05 | $25.05 |

## How Fees Work

- **Deducted from payment amount.** The recipient receives the payment minus the protocol fee. No separate fee transaction is needed.
- **Fixed fee on every transaction.** The $0.05 fixed fee applies regardless of volume.
- **Smart wallet gas included.** For smart wallet payments, the paymaster gas cost is included within the fee — users never need native gas tokens.
- **Collected by the protocol.** Fees are collected by the Relayer contract and sent to the protocol fee recipient.
