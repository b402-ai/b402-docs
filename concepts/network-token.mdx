## Network Support

**b402** verifies and settles payments on the **BNB Chain**, serving as its primary execution network for gasless and agent-native transactions.  

Facilitators process and confirm on-chain settlements through the B402Relayer contract, ensuring secure and verifiable payment flows.


## Token Support

**b402** currently supports the following assets on the BNB Chain:

- **BNB**
- **USDT** 
- **USD1**

Additional BEP-20 token support will be added as the protocol evolves.
