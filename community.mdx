The **b402 community** is where operators, integrators, and curious builders come together to shape the future of the protocol.  
Whether you’re building agent systems, integrating payments, or just exploring what’s next — this is where ideas become standards.

Join our community Discord to stay up to date with new releases, share ideas, or get real-time support from the core team and moderators. We keep a close eye on the support channels to make sure questions never go unanswered.
